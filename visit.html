Plan Your Visit

Everything you need to know before you go

Opening Hours

Saturday: 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM

Sunday: 10:00 AM - 6:00 PM

Monday: 11:00 AM - 5:00 PM

Note: Some markets may have slightly different hours. Most dealers are closed Tuesday through Friday.

Prices & Bargaining

Prices range from a few euros to thousands for high-end antiques. Polite bargaining is expected in most markets, especially for higher-priced items.

Many dealers accept credit cards, but it's good to have cash for smaller purchases and better bargaining power.

Food & Amenities

Several cafés and restaurants are scattered throughout the markets, offering everything from quick snacks to full meals.

Public restrooms are available near the main entrances of each market (small fee may apply).

Visitor Tips

Wear Comfortable Shoes

The market covers over 7 hectares with uneven cobblestone paths. You'll be doing a lot of walking!

Bring Cash

While many dealers accept cards, cash is king for bargaining and smaller purchases.

Ask Before Photographing

Some dealers prefer you ask permission before taking photos of their items.

Come Early

The best finds often go quickly. Arriving when markets open gives you first pick.

Learn Basic French

While many dealers speak English, knowing basic French phrases can enhance your experience.

Grab a Map

Pick up a free map at the information booth to navigate the maze of markets.
